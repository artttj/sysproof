MECHANISM (the real story):
  sysproof made kimi claim DONE on ALL 30 cells (vs 24 control / 23 placebo),
  eliminating the model's 6-7 natural NOT_DONE abstentions. Those forced
  declarations split into BOTH more passes (17 vs 15) AND more false-dones.
  The "evidence gate" framing pushed a weaker model to assert completion
  confidently rather than hedge -> net effect was more confident wrong "done".

CONSISTENT WITH the Claude runs: sysproof never reduces false-done. On a
weaker open model it numerically increases it by suppressing honest hedging.
